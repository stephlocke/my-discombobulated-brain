## Safeguarding

We are committed to safeguarding children, young people, and adults at risk. Our safeguarding policy outlines our approach and responsibilities.

### Reporting concerns
If you have a safeguarding concern, contact us immediately. We take all reports seriously and act in accordance with UK law.
