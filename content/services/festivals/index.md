At My Discombobulated Brain, we believe festivals should be spaces where everyone feels safe, supported, and free to enjoy the energy of the event.We’re here to make sure that mental health and well-being are prioritised, safeguarding your guests and providing the support they need to fully embrace your event.

- Guidance and advice for organisers,
- tips and support for attendees,
- a register of mental-health-concious events.
